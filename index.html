Why This Property
Audubon address at below-market entry — comprehensive renovation turns it into the most defensible asset on the list.
This property sits in 99208 (Audubon/Five Mile) — Spokane's strongest resale zip — at a list price $75–125K below comparable turnkey homes on the same streets. The thesis: buy the worst house on the best block, invest $160–200K in full renovation (finished basement, 3rd bath, updated kitchen and baths, new flooring), and emerge with a custom home at below-replacement cost in a zip that sells even in down markets. Finished product would be priced $700–750K against comparable turnkey sales.

The caution: at $610K all-in, you need a $760K+ sale to clear transaction costs on a 5-year exit. This is a 10-year hold or a home you'd genuinely want to stay in. If either of you walks in and it feels like the place, the calculus changes entirely.
